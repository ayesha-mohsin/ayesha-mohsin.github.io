About Me

I'm Ayesha Mohsin — I blend the rigor of data analysis with the creativity of machine learning to build predictive models, extract meaningful insights, and design systems that are technically sound, socially conscious, and humane.

As a machine learning research assistant, I built and evaluated models — including neural networks, SVMs, and Mask R-CNN — for defense-funded applications supported by a $5.5M ARL grant. I've worked across the data pipeline using Python, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI to turn messy data into meaningful outcomes.

I'm passionate about the ethical advancement of AI — driven by the belief that innovation should serve human well-being. I aim to build systems that prioritize empathy and human-centered outcomes over engagement metrics and profit margins. Whether coding or designing, I'm always exploring the intersection of art, logic, and human experience.
